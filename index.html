About the HospitalGovernment healthcare, close to home

Named in honour of the celebrated Indian filmmaker Tapan Sinha, the hospital has served the Ashok Nagar – Tollygunge catchment for decades under the administrative control of the Department of Health & Family Welfare, Government of West Bengal. From routine OPD consultations to emergency trauma care, institutional deliveries and diagnostics, the hospital is committed to accessible, dignified public healthcare.

The hospital is empanelled under Swasthya Sathi for cashless treatment and works closely with referral hospitals across Kolkata for tertiary care.
