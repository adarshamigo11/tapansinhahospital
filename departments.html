Departments & Services

Clinical departments, outpatient schedules and specialised services offered

Patients are requested to collect the OPD token from the registration counter before 12:30 PM. Emergency services are available 24x7 irrespective of OPD timings.

OPD Registration Process

Step 1

Collect a token from the registration counter with a valid ID / Swasthya Sathi card.

Step 2

Wait in the designated OPD waiting area; tokens are called in sequence.

Step 3

Consult the doctor; collect prescribed medicines from the hospital pharmacy counter.

Referral & Higher Centres

Patients requiring tertiary or super-speciality care are referred, as per protocol, to higher government medical college hospitals within the Kolkata region, with referral slips issued by the treating physician free of cost.
