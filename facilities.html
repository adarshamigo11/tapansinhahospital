Emergency & Critical Care

The Emergency & Trauma department functions 24 hours a day, all seven days of the week, equipped for triage, resuscitation and stabilisation of critical patients prior to admission or referral. An Intensive/High Dependency care unit supports post-operative and critical patients requiring close monitoring.
