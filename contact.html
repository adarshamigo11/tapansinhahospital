How to Reach

  • By Metro: Nearest station – Netaji / Kavi Nazrul on the Kolkata Metro; hospital is a short auto/taxi ride away.
  • By Bus: Multiple WBTC and private bus routes serve Tollygunge and Regent Park; alight near Ashok Nagar.
  • By Road: Located on Mahanayak Uttamkumar Sarani, well connected to Tollygunge Circular Road.
  • Parking: Limited visitor parking available within hospital premises.
